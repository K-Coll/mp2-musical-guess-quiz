Musical Guess!

Question

Test your pop music knowledge!

You will be presented with the lyrics from a well-known pop song. The aim of the quiz is to correctly identify the song title and artist from the four available options.

Upon completion, you will be presented with both your final score and the amount of time taken.

You will also have the option to play the quiz again - try to beat your previous score or see if you can race against your friends for the most correct answers in the quickest amount of time!.

You have 90 seconds to complete 10 questions - Good Luck!
